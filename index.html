MIRICLE

MIRICLE is software for the MIRI test team. The aim of MIRICLE is to provide an easy to install and easy to use python environment with everything needed to investigate the test results of MIRI.

Read more

MIRI

The Mid-Infrared Instrument (MIRI) on JWST will provide direct imaging and medium resolution spectroscopy (R~3000) over the wavelength range 5 - 28.3 micron, coronagraphic imaging at 10.65, 11.4, 15.5 and 23 micron, and low resolution spectroscopy (R~100) over the wavelength range 5 - 10 micron.

Version 2.1 of MIRICLE

Version 2.1 of the installation script makes the installation of IRAF optional. Only when installing using the parameter --iraf, IRAF will be installed. You will need 4 gigabyte of disk space during installation, when IRAF is not installed, only 2 gigabyte will remain after the installion.
